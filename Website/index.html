About SafeBike

The website aims to provide an interactive tool through which tourists, students, and other Londoners can explore and inform themselves about bicycle theft and the risk levels of different boroughs.

How SafeBike Helps You

Thousands of Bikes are stolen in London each year and currently it is mostly recorded through numerical figures. SafeBike innovatively presents this information through a visually interactive map making it easier identify patterns and trends of bicycle theft within different LSOA's of London.
