About SafeBike

The website aims to provide an interactive tool through which tourists, students, and other Londoners can explore and inform themselves about bicycle theft and the risk levels of different boroughs.

How SafeBike Helps You

Thousands of Bikes are stolen in London each year and currently this crime is mostly recorded through numerical figures. SafeBike innovatively presents this information through a visually interactive map making it easier identify patterns and trends of bicycle theft within different LSOA's of London Boroughs.

Find The Safest Parking Spots

SafeBike will inform users when they enter an area with high levels of bicycle theft, helping them choose the safest place to park their bikes.

Accurate and Reliable Routing

SafeBike has a routing function to allow you to navigate to safer parking spots within the borough you are in.
